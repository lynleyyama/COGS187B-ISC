ISC Job Fair 2017

Mission: The Sustainability Job Fair aims to match students' talents with opportunities in the sustainability sector.

We hope to empower UC San Diego students with the knowledge and creativity to meet environmental and social challenges as well as make a sustainable lifestyle and future accessible. ​


2017 Sustainable Job Fair

April 26 from 10AM - 2PM

Price Center Ballroom West Ballroom AB (2nd Floor) ​


-Eliya Baron Lopez

Sustainability Job Fair Coordinator

Director of Community and Outreach (Student Sustainability Collective)





WHAT:

UCSD's second annual Green Talks. This FREE event is a TED-styled sustainability talk, where we aim to celebrate a greener future and spark discussion about sustainability. ​

Six speakers will be covering different perspectives of sustainability, along with our presenters there will be an interactive Q and A panel, networking session, organic and vegan snacks from Trilogy Sanctuary, a spectacular aerial yoga performance, sustainable product resourcess, free yoga passes, and sustainable raffle prizes! ​





RecycleMania: February 5 - April 1, 2017

RecycleMania is an eight week long nationwide competition across universities in the United States and in Canada which aims to promote waste-reduction activities to campus communities.

Encompassing the ideas of reducing, reusing and recycling, each week engages the students in a new activity in order to help foster an environmentally conscious mentally about waste disposal.

Every week there will be a new winner or team of winners who win a fun, sustainable prize! The nationwide winner will be announced after the competition is over, and is based on which university recycles the most on a per-capita basis. ​
