MISSION STATEMENT

ISC welcomes all of the environmental student organizations on campus who encompass the same goal: sustainability. The goal of ISC is to bring together all of the sustainability organizations in order to foster an environment of growth and productivity on our campus.

WHAT WE VALUE

WASTE

To protect future generations, we must cut pollution and expand clean energy.

WATER

Clean water sustains our communities, economies, ecosystems, and health.

ENERGY

Expanding clean energy protects our health, air, climate, and last wild places.

SOCIAL JUSTICE

Developing countries deserve to be involved in fair trade.
